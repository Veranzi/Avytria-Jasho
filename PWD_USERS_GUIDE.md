## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### Voice Not Working?

1. **Check microphone permission**
   - Say: "Settings"
   - Say: "Permissions"
   - Say: "Allow microphone"

2. **Speak clearly**
   - Speak at normal pace
   - Avoid background noise

3. **Switch language**
   - Say: "Switch language"
   - Try English or Swahili

### Can't Hear App?

1. **Check volume**
   - Press volume up button

2. **Enable voice mode**
   - Tap speaker icon (top-right)

3. **Restart voice assistant**
   - Say: "Restart" or tap mic button twice
